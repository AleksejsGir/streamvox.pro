## üîç HOW AI DISCOVERS PRODUCTS

### AI Decision-Making Process

**1. Training Data (Historical)**
- Content published before AI's knowledge cutoff
- Articles, reviews, forum discussions
- Product documentation
- Authoritative source mentions

**2. Web Search (Real-Time)**
- When AI doesn't know product ‚Üí searches web
- Reads current articles, reviews
- Checks official websites
- Validates information quality

**3. Quality Signals**
- Source authority (TechCrunch > random blog)
- User reviews quantity and quality
- Documentation completeness
- Transparency (honest about limitations)

## üö´ WHAT NOT TO DO

### Anti-Patterns (Will Hurt AI Discovery)

**‚ùå DON'T:**

1. **Buy Fake Reviews**
   - AI detects patterns
   - Destroys credibility
   - Can get banned

2. **Keyword Stuffing**
   - Makes content unreadable
   - Google penalizes
   - AI ignores low-quality content

3. **Exaggerate Claims**
   - "Best in the world"
   - "10x better than competitors"
   - AI fact-checks and flags

4. **Copy-Paste Content**
   - Duplicate content penalty
   - AI recognizes plagiarism
   - No value to users

5. **Spam Links**
   - Posting same link everywhere
   - Reddit bans, social media flags
   - Negative signal to AI

6. **Ignore Negative Feedback**
   - Not responding to reviews
   - Dismissing criticism
   - AI sees lack of engagement

7. **Inconsistent Information**
   - Different pricing on different sites
   - Conflicting feature descriptions
   - AI gets confused, won't recommend
